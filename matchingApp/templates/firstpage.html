Achieving Fairness and Stability in Many-to-One Matchings

Problem Overview

The algorithms we will represent address the challenge of achieving fairness in stable many-to-one matchings, particularly in scenarios where both sides of the matching have preferences, and these preferences are expressed using cardinal valuations. Traditional research on matching problems has focused predominantly on stability and fairness separately, often under the assumption of ordinal preferences.

Algorithms

  • FaSt (Fairness and Stability): Find a leximin optimal stable matching under ranked isometric valuations.
  • FaSt-Gen (Fairness and Stability - General): Find a matching for the general ranked valuations setting.

Based on: "On Achieving Fairness and Stability in Many-to-One Matchings" by Shivika Narang, Arpita Biswas, and Y Narahari (2022) Link to the Paper
